ABOUT ME


Junior Graphic Designer, born in the 2000s, specialized in UX/UI Designer, with a creative approach and a passion for designing intuitive, accessible and user experience-focused digital solutions.

I completed a theoretical and practical master's degree at start2impact, during which I carried out 14 projects developing different UX/UI Design skills using tools such as Figma and Sketch.

My previous experience as an office clerk allowed me to hone a strong orientation towards understanding customer needs, solving complex problems and effective communication, skills that I now apply in digital design.

Today I think that the right job for me is a dynamic, creative job, always evolving and capable of encouraging me every day.
