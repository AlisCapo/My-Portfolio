ABOUT ME


I was born in 2000, I graduated from the High School of Applied Sciences and immediately after I worked for 2 years in the municipal environment as an employee.

After that experience I understood that it wasn't the right path for me, as it didn't give me stimulation and took away all my energy.

Today I think that the right job for me is a dynamic, creative job, always evolving and capable of encouraging me every day.

This is why I decided to undertake the UX/UI Design path with start2impact University.

My goal is to work as a Freelancer in this field and one day open my own business and also have my own team.
